Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Layered Up ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. This policy applies to all of our platforms including TikTok Shop, YouTube, Etsy, Telegram, Pinterest, X (Twitter), Reddit, and Amazon.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Order information (name, shipping address, email) when you purchase through TikTok Shop, Etsy, or other platforms — this is collected and processed by the respective platform, not directly by us
  • Messages you send to us through any platform
  • Reviews and comments you post on our content

Information collected automatically:

  • Platform analytics provided by TikTok, YouTube, Etsy, etc. (views, engagement, demographics in aggregate — we cannot identify individual users from this data)
  • UTM tracking parameters on affiliate links (these track which platform led to a click, not who clicked)
  • Amazon Associates reporting (aggregate sales data — we cannot see individual purchaser identities)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not directly collect credit card or payment information — all payments are processed by the selling platform (TikTok, Etsy, Amazon, etc.)
  • We do not use cookies (we do not operate a standalone website that tracks visitors)
  • We do not purchase or obtain personal data from third parties
  • We do not collect information from children (see COPPA section below)

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we have access to in order to:

  • Fulfill and ship orders placed through our storefronts
  • Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our products based on feedback and reviews
  • Analyze aggregate sales and engagement data to optimize our content and product offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform requirements

5. Affiliate Links & Tracking

Our content contains affiliate links to products on Amazon and other retailers. When you click an affiliate link:

  • The retailer (e.g., Amazon) may place cookies on your device per their own privacy policy
  • We may earn a commission on qualifying purchases at no additional cost to you
  • Our affiliate links contain UTM parameters that tell us which platform the click came from (e.g., "from Telegram" vs "from TikTok") — these do not identify you personally
  • We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of Amazon, TikTok, or other third-party platforms

As an Amazon Associate, Layered Up earns from qualifying purchases.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With shipping carriers to fulfill orders (name and address only)
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Layered Up or others

7. Data Retention

Order information is retained as required by the selling platform and applicable tax laws. Customer communications are retained for as long as needed to resolve inquiries. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Contact section).

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you

Layered Up does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our products and content are not directed at children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To make a CCPA request, contact us at the address below.

14. European Users (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: We process personal data on the basis of contract performance (fulfilling orders), legitimate interests (improving our products and services), and consent (where applicable, such as subscribing to our Telegram channels).
  • Right to access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to restrict processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.
